Detroit Space Academy

Our Vision

We are Detroit Space Academy, an educational initiative focused on bridging the gap between black talent and the aerospace industry. Detroit Space Academy is a brand that stands for innovation, discovery, curiosity, and up-to date industry knowledge.

We envision a world where underrepresented students:

  • Pursue STEM fields in higher education.
  • Are aware of the many opportunities in the burgeoning aerospace and space industry.
  • Are passionate and excited about pursuing higher education.
  • Eagerly give back to the city of Detroit.
