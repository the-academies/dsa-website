What is Detroit Space Academy?

Detroit Space Academy is an educational initiative focused on bridging the gap between black talent and the aerospace industry. We stand for discovery, curiosity, and up-to-date industry knowledge.  


We want to provide low-resource classrooms with science experiment kits to drive students into the aerospace industry, to be a part of this up-and-coming field that they have been historically excluded from.

Our Vision & Product

Detroit Space Academy wants to change the face of space.

Science Experiment Kits

Each science experiment kit is customized for the size and needs of the classroom. It contains a themed science experiment, essential materials, and space science information.

Engaging Experiments

Experiments within a kit contain themed science experiments. These experiments bring science topics to life by providing relatable and engaging experiences.

Encouraging Students

Our kits are a new take on science education. While adhering to state education standards, we tell stories that we hope inspire students to pursue a career in STEM.
