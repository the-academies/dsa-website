Our Mission and Goal

Through our academies, we wish to provide STEM exposure to students in the Detroit and metro Detroit area, inspire them to pursue higher education, teach about leadership, quality and excellence through experience that enforce a core STEM curriclum.

    We envision a world where:
  • where black students pursue STEM fields/higher ed
  • where students are aware of the many opp in AERO/SPACE fields
  • where students eagerly give back to the city of Detroit
  • where students care about their education
